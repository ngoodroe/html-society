Welcome to the Society of HTML Purists!

A dedicated group of people who believe that the internet is best with any of the junk and clutter that is CSS and JS.

How many times have you ever wanted to just go to a webpage and it takes minutes, sometime hours to load? That's because of all the bulky, heavy scripts and animations and positoning that has to be done before you ever get to see the words on the screen
